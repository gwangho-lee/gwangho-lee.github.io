Intro

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University (SNU).

I believe the computational power of computer systems is becoming a major bottleneck due to the rapidly widening gap between the performance of a single core and the performance required by the latest applications (e.g., Hyperscale AI). My primary research interest is to address this disparity, which includes multicore computing, domain-specific architecture, system software, and networking. I have undergone several research projects spanning digital circuit design, computer architecture, and computer systems.

I wish to further my research while focusing on the following goals:

  • Design domain-specific accelerators with an emphasis on scalability built from the ground up with large-scale systems in mind
  • Pursue ways to effectively orchestrate numerous cores with tight hardware/software co-design
  • Devise lean and direct implementations to eliminate the inefficiency accumulated over the layers of computer systems

I was fortunate to work with Prof. Dongsuk Jeon on digital circuit design, Prof. Jangwoo Kim on computer systems simulation, and Prof. Jung Ho Ahn on computer architecture during my undergraduate studies.
